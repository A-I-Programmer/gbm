Four Masterpieces

Scientific Renaissance

Like master artists discovering new pigments, we support bold researchers who paint breakthrough discoveries on the canvas of human knowledge, creating masterpieces of scientific understanding.

Nature's Gallery

Preserving Earth's living artwork through innovative conservation, we protect the planet's natural masterpieces while inspiring sustainable harmony between humanity and nature's creativity.

Healing Arts

Transforming patient care into an art form, we support medical innovations that heal not just bodies but spirits, creating beautiful stories of recovery and hope.

Bay Area Tapestry

Weaving together the cultural and natural threads of the San Francisco Bay Area, preserving its unique character as a living artwork for future generations to cherish.
