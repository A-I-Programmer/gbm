Creating Positive Outcomes for Future Generations

The Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation fosters path-breaking scientific discovery, environmental conservation, patient care improvements, and preservation of the special character of the San Francisco Bay Area.

Explore Our Mission Request Funding

Our Four Focus Areas

Scientific Discovery

Supporting bold scientific research that challenges conventional thinking and opens new frontiers of knowledge. We invest in exceptional researchers and transformative ideas that have the potential to benefit humanity.

Environmental Conservation

Protecting and preserving the natural world through innovative conservation strategies, sustainable practices, and cutting-edge environmental research that addresses climate change and biodiversity loss.

Patient Care Improvement

Advancing patient care through innovative healthcare solutions, improved treatment outcomes, and transformative medical technologies that enhance the quality of life for patients and their families.

Bay Area Character

Preserving the unique character and natural beauty of the San Francisco Bay Area while supporting sustainable growth and maintaining the region's cultural and environmental heritage.
